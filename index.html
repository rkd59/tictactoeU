Quick Rules

  • Your move's cell position sends opponent to that mini-board
  • Win a mini-board to claim it on the mega-grid
  • If target board is full/won, opponent plays anywhere
  • Win 3 mini-boards in a row on the mega-grid

How to Play

Ultimate Tic Tac Toe — a deceptively deep twist on the classic.

The Setup

9 mini-boards arranged in a 3×3 mega-grid. Each mini-board is its own 3×3 tic-tac-toe game.

The Twist

The cell position of your move determines which mini-board your opponent must play in next. Play top-left? Opponent goes to the top-left mini-board. This creates chains of forced moves.

The Escape

If your target mini-board is already won or full, you may play in any open mini-board. Skilled players use this to escape bad positions.

The Win

Win three mini-boards in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on the mega-grid to win the game.
